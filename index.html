How It Works

Augmented reality blends digital content with the real world, enhancing your perception and interaction.
We use a combination of AR and WebRTC technology to share your screen into your Reality.

  • Step1: Open link: Start share desktop
  • Step2: Click to "Start Sharing display"
  • Step3: Choose display or browser TAB to share
  • Step4: Scan QR code with your mobile
